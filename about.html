About Me

My name is Stephanie Arteaga. I am 21 years old from Northern California. Currently I am studying at CSU Monterey Bay for Communication Design, with a minor in computer science.

I have always been interested in Design since I was an adolescent. I learned how to do CSS & HTML coding when I was 12 years old. I taught myself Adobe Photoshop as well.

Since I had such a passion for creating at a small age, I decided to finally start studying Design again. I look forward to learning new things and designing new projects.
